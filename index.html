Educational Field Trips

We take kids on educational field trips around the world, allowing them to explore new cultures, historical landmarks, and natural wonders, broadening their horizons and enriching their understanding of the world.

Science & Innovation

We immerse children in the scientific realm, offering challenges and hands-on experiences in areas like mathematics, artificial intelligence, and robotics, fostering curiosity and problem-solving skills critical for the future.

Sports Activities

We encourage kids to participate in a variety of sports including fencing, swimming, and soccer, helping them build physical strength, confidence, and a healthy lifestyle.

School Support

We support the schools where these children study, providing funding for resources and opportunities to ensure they have the tools needed to thrive academically and personally.
